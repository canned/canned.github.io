Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

The Simple Version: Fifty Two Pickup does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Period.

1. Data Collection

Fifty Two Pickup is built with a "Privacy by Design" philosophy. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), device identifiers, or usage statistics. All app progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising frameworks. No data is ever transmitted to external servers.

3. Accessibility Transparency

In accordance with 2026 App Store standards, Fifty Two Pickup is designed to be compatible with iOS Accessibility features including VoiceOver and Dynamic Type. No biometric or health data is accessed or required for these features to function.

4. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect any data, our app is fully compliant with COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please reach out via the Support page.
